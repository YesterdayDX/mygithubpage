Research Interest

I am fasincated by one research question: how to efficiently explore the indoor white spaces (vacant TV channels)? I ground this long-term goal to the following problems:

  • Deploy spectrum detectors at a set of proper indoor locations.
  • Reconstruct the indoor white space availabities based on the measurement of spectrum detectors.
